where are the traffic signals and street lamps?

the ask

  • predict the locations of all traffic signals and streetlamps in a given CMA
  • a verified asset must be found within a 0.2581 mile radius at least 75% of the time

the model

  • generates between 200,000 to 400,000 assets per MSA
  • model runtime: 35min (thanks to Zac A)
  • traffic signal model complexity
    • road class
    • speed
    • intersections
  • streetlight model complexity
    • road class
    • streetlight spacing averages

the results

  • model ave accuracy: 80% (hi:95% - lo:50%)
  • cities passed: 20
  • cities failed: 1 (Boston)
